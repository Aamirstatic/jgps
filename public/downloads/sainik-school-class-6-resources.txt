4. JGPS EXPERT PREPARATION TIPS FOR SAINIK SCHOOL
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Speed is Key in Math: Since Math represents 50% of the score, practice calculations daily. Memorize tables up to 25 and squares up to 30.
2. No Negative Marking: Teach your child to attempt all 125 questions. Do not leave any circle blank on the OMR sheet.
3. OMR Practice: 10% of qualified children fail because of bad bubble filling. Practice filling OMR sheets every week.
