Thank you for requesting access to the JGPS Rashtriya Military School (RMS) Study Pack. Below is the CET exam pattern, syllabus checklist, and expert preparation strategies for admissions in Class 6.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. RMS CET CLASS 6 WRITTEN EXAM PATTERN
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Total Marks: 200 (Objective MCQs, Duration: 2.5 Hours)

* Intelligence Test (Reasoning): 50 Questions | 50 Marks (25%)
* Mathematics: 50 Questions | 50 Marks (25%)
* General Knowledge & Current Affairs: 50 Questions | 50 Marks (25%)
* English: 50 Questions | 50 Marks (25%)
  *CRITICAL RULE:* English marks are only **qualifying in nature** (minimum 35% or 17.5 marks required). English marks are NOT added to the final merit list, but you must pass English! The final merit list is based out of 150 Marks (Math, Intelligence, GK).

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. SUBJECT-WISE SYLLABUS CHECKLIST (Class 5 Standards)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
* INTELLIGENCE (50 Marks):
  [ ] Visual and spatial reasoning (embedded figures, folding paper)
  [ ] Coding-decoding, logical reasoning, and relationship puzzles
  [ ] Number and symbol series, direction test
* MATHEMATICS (50 Marks):
  [ ] Number system, Fractions, Decimals
  [ ] HCF & LCM, Ratio & Proportion, Percentage
  [ ] Simple Interest, Profit & Loss, Average
  [ ] Perimeter, Area and Volume, Temperature
* GENERAL KNOWLEDGE (50 Marks):
  [ ] History of India, Freedom struggle highlights
  [ ] Geography basics, solar system, rivers & mountains
  [ ] General Science, human body, vitamins & health
  [ ] Current affairs, defense forces, sports & awards
* ENGLISH (50 Marks - Qualifying Only):
  [ ] Sentence structure, nouns, pronouns, verbs, adjectives
  [ ] Synonyms, Antonyms, One-word substitutions
  [ ] Spelling tests, Idioms and Phrases

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. JGPS EXPERT RMS PREPARATION TIPS
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Focus on Math & Intelligence: Since English is only qualifying, the final selection depends heavily on your Math and Intelligence scores. Daily practice of logic puzzles is crucial.
2. Speed Management: 200 questions in 150 minutes means you have less than 45 seconds per question! High speed and mental alertness are essential.
3. Medical & Physical Fitness: Military schools have strict health standards. Ensure your child is physically active, has correct vision, and has no bone or posture issues.
