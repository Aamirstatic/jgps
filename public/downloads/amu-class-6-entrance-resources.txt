1. AMU CLASS 6 WRITTEN EXAM PATTERN & SELECTION PROCESS
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Written Test: 85 Marks (Objective MCQs, Duration: 2 Hours)
Interview: 15 Marks (Conducted for students qualifying the written cutoff)

The Written Paper is divided into two sections:
* SECTION-I: Languages (English, Hindi, Urdu) - 25 Marks
  *CRITICAL RULE:* Student must score at least 40% (10 marks out of 25) in this section to qualify. If they score less than 10, their whole paper is disqualified even if they score 60/60 in Section-II!
* SECTION-II: Mathematics (30 Marks) & Science (30 Marks) - 60 Marks

3. JGPS EXPERT AMU PREPARATION TIPS
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Focus on Urdu Grammar: Most English-medium students struggle to get the minimum qualifying 10 marks in Section-I due to Urdu. Daily reading and spelling practice is mandatory.
2. Conceptual Science: AMU does not ask direct questions. They ask application-based questions (e.g., "Why does a seed not grow inside a closed jar?"). Focus on NCERT concepts.
3. Interview Prep: 15 marks of interview are highly decisive. Practice self-introduction, favor-subject questions, and basic GK at home.
