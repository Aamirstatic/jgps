2. JNVST Detailed Mental Ability Test (MAT) Workbook Checklist
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Mental Ability represents 50% of the marks (50 Marks, 40 Questions).
Practice the following figures daily:
- Odd-Man Out (विजातीय आकृति)
- Figure Matching (समान आकृति)
- Pattern Completion (आकृति पूरक)
- Figure Series Completion (आकृति श्रृंखला)
- Analogy (सादृश्यता)
- Geometrical Figure Completion (ज्यामितीय आकृति: वर्ग/त्रिभुज/वृत्त)
- Mirror Imaging (दर्पण प्रतिबिम्ब)
- Punched Hole Pattern (कागज मोड़ना और काटना)
- Space Visualization (आकृति निर्माण)
- Embedded Figures (छिपी हुई आकृति)

3. Mathematics Arithmetic Test Core Formulas
----------------------------------------------------------------------
- Number System (संख्या प्रणाली)
- LCM & HCF (लघुत्तम समापवर्त्य और महत्तम समापवर्तक)
- Fractions & Decimals (भिन्न और दशमलव)
- Simple Interest: SI = (P * R * T) / 100
- Profit & Loss: Profit = SP - CP | Loss = CP - SP
- Speed, Distance & Time: Distance = Speed * Time
- Perimeter & Area: Rectangle Area = L * W | Square Area = Side * Side
